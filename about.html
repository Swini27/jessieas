"Some dreams take flight long before they find their runway."

Wing Commander Shikha Chaudhary (Retd.), after a distinguished career with the Indian Air Force, always knew what came next — not a quiet retirement, but a new kind of mission. One built around people, warmth, and the simple joy of good food shared in great company.

"I'm a very social person, and I always wanted to create a space for people to come in and meet new people after my retirement. This was always my post-retirement plan."

— Wing Commander Shikha Chaudhary (Retd.)

That dream became Jessiea's Cafe. With the same precision and dedication that defined her years in uniform, Shikha turned a vision into one of Mumbai's most exciting new dining destinations.

The Food

Tucked in the heart of 7 Bungalows, Andheri West, Jessiea's brings together two of the world's most beloved food cultures — Japanese and Italian — under one roof. From delicately crafted Japanese-inspired dishes to soul-warming Italian classics, every plate is designed to surprise, comfort, and delight. Whether you're a fan of clean umami flavours or hearty pasta and antipasti, there's a dish here that was made for you.

The Vibe

Jessiea's isn't just somewhere you come to eat — it's somewhere you come to feel something. The space is warm, thoughtfully designed, and unapologetically Instagrammable, wrapped in an energy that makes you want to linger long after your last bite. It's the kind of place where strangers become regulars, and regulars become friends.

The Live Lounge

At the heart of Jessiea's beats a stage. The Jessiea's Live Lounge hosts regular, intimate, unplugged, soulful evenings featuring live performances from acclaimed artists District — think raw vocals, acoustic magic, and heart-touching melodies filling the room on a Friday night. Past evenings have featured playback singers like Simran Choudhary, known for her work in blockbusters like Stree 2 and Dhurandhar. With limited seats and big talent, these are evenings you don't want to miss.

Whether you're dropping in for a quiet lunch, a celebration dinner, or a live music night that stays with you for days — Jessiea's is where good vibes live.
